Dahrk The Artist is a UK based independent hip-hop musician from ODE-IRELE, Ondo State, Nigeria. Whilst affiliated to his record label, Play Plug as well as a collective, The Midnight Crew. His music journey started off freesylong to youtube beats in his Univerity Room. He is best known for his rhyme style and cathcy hooks
